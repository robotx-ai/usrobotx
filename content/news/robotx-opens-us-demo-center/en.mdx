RobotX has opened its first US demo center at its Irvine, California
headquarters. The space is built for hands-on evaluation — prospective
customers can walk robots through their own scenarios, review live spatial
perception output, and rehearse deployments before committing to a pilot.

## What the space covers

The demo center is organized around four stations, each mapped to a RobotX
solution track:

- **Inspection** — routine patrol, anomaly triage, and operator handoff.
- **Cleaning** — autonomous route coverage and service-quality metrics.
- **Logistics** — intra-facility material flow and multi-robot coordination.
- **Service** — guest-facing delivery and hospitality interaction.

## How to visit

Visits are available by appointment with the RobotX sales and solutions team.
Sessions run ninety minutes and include a live platform walkthrough, a scenario
tailored to the visiting team, and a working review of the data the robot
captures during the run.

The Irvine demo center will also host quarterly partner briefings for
distributors and integrators across the US footprint.
