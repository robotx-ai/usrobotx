FFM: Built by the Minds Behind the Most Advanced Embodied AI Solutions of the Last Decade

FieldAI Research Institute (FAIRI)

Pioneering Real-World Foundation Model Deployment

FieldAI is at the forefront of foundation model research and deployment, bridging the gap between theoretical breakthroughs and real-world applications. By pioneering advancements in the science of risk- and uncertainty-aware foundation models, we have enabled scalable and robust deployments into complex, real-world environments. Leveraging our massive multimodal datasets, we showcase the transformative potential of foundation models in our numerous active customer deployments today.

Unlocking the Next Frontier of Embodied AI
Our research lab is at the leading edge of foundation models and embodied intelligence.
