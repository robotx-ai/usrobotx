FieldAI is already working with many leading companies within their respective industries, many of whom are now able to deploy robots in their facilities for the first time. Our solutions are helping customers gain a much deeper understanding of their operations, by collecting and interpreting vast amounts of data across their job sites and turning it into actionable insights.

Automate Your Industry

Through Field Foundation Models, FieldAI is transforming the way businesses understand and act on the conditions of their job sites. Our hardware-agnostic autonomy software turns nearly any type of mobile robot into a data collection powerhouse, giving our customers unprecedented insight into what is and isn’t working well within their facilities.
